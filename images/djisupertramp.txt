Introducing @CaptainYvon! For the past 2 years, we've been thinking about a common project around the outdoors and creating images with my good friend @santadenn. It took us time but we finally found how we wanted this project to be. It had to be visual, passionate, authentic, adventurous, green to celebrate and take care of the outdoors. It had to be all of this in order to be right and to reflect our mindset, our lifestyle and our passion. @CaptainYvon was born. Since then @exploremarco has joined the team and we're now 3 people behind this non-profit association. Our goal is to share our love for the ocean, mountains, rivers, lakes, waves, glaciers, wildlife and so much more that can be found outdoors. We want to help people re-discovering nature, capturing true adventures with strong visuals and storytelling and raise awareness around our ecosystem, leave no trace and take greater care of our fragile playground that is Earth. Whether it's hiking, surfing, kayaking, traveling, mountain biking, skiing or snowshoeing we all have a different way to connect with nature. Nature is beautiful. And we want to celebrate it and use our visual skills to tell stories to show the good and the bad so we can all live great adventures out there leaving no footprint behind.
