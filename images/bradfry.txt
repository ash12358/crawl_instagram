A blue hour photo of the jetty at Grange beach in Adelaide. I had planned to take this shot further down the jetty but it was too windy for the 30 second exposure I wanted to capture the movement in the clouds. I think the winds up higher we’re actually quite slow as the clouds moved very little in this frame (much less than I had hoped). First time using the Sony remote app on my iPhone which was amazing. Allowed me to keep my shadow out of the frame and review a high quality jpg on the Retina display which is much easier than in camera.
