Hi! This is me. Friends, people I meet and collaborators often ask me what I do, so I thought I'd tell y'all about it. For the last 6+ years I've worked at @frog_design, a global design and strategy consultancy. My role is Principal Designer. This means I lead design and research teams on projects, from the early investigative phases of figuring out what problem to solve, to going out into the world to interview and observe the people we are designing for, to identify their underlying needs and behaviors, and to bring insights and opportunities to our clients in the form of useful, usable product and service ideas. We call it #frogDR. This is the best part of my job. I travel a lot to do this work - pretty much wherever our clients are. Like this month, I'm in Malawi working to uncover the needs of smallholder farmers and gaps in mobile services available to farmers throughout the country. The second best part of my job is that I get to spend quality time in incredible places that afford life-enriching experiences, and almost endless opportunities to learn about people. And the 12 mile journey through Boquillas ahead! And the wind on the water must certainly let up, right? Spoiler alert: it got worse. But it also just got better, know what I mean?
https://scontent-sit4-1.cdninstagram.com/vp/7b12d7bb3a51a07bc9838b8b25429950/5BCB041B/t51.2885-15/e35/14733333_1286602061390582_2191668055338123264_n.jpg
Three days and 34 miles down the Rio Grande and damn! So full of nature and inspiration and beauty and wonderful people I just want to go back and do it all over again. What a perfect way to spend thanksgiving, reflecting on all of the things I have to be thankful for and planning to build a fantastic future filled with more days like this.
