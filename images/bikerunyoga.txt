Yes, I DID just fix the toilet at the studio. About a week ago after both the inexpensive plungers broke trying to get the toilet to flush I resorted to all sorts of things. Don't ask. It flushed but the bowl didn't fill, the water would sink down to a very low level and just sit there. The next day I tried a better plunger and nothing. Then over the next several days I looked up a bunch of stuff on google and watched several videos, read several plumber chat boards and decided that something must be stuck in the line and that an auger would be necessary. Today I went to Home Depot, bought better plungers and an auger. It took some work and nearly half a container of bleach down the drain hole to the main line but I did it! Toilet now flushes properly. Uber happy. ‪#‎studiolife‬
