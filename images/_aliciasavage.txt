Throughout this summer the public art installation WHY CHROMOSOME, created by my dear friend Lindsay Baer, has been continuously vandalized - this past weekend to the point of complete dismantlement. Which is somewhat impressive given it was filled with concrete, but making it even more disheartening that someone would act with so much destructive effort. Lindsay had been commissioned by the city in its production - receiving her first grant as an artist. Despite it all this young artist has repaired it each time, even now while having to apply fiber glass to adhere the sculptures bodily ligaments. She is relentless in her determination to always rebuild. Proud of you @baer.naeked !
