Successfully Summit the Kedarkantha Peak! I was preparing for this trek since last 5 months with adequate training every week. The Trek is listed as Easy to Moderate but nothing remains the same at the Himalayas. On our first day of the trek, the Himalayan weather turns sudden turn and the second snowfall of this season starts. The snowfall turns the drastically change the surrounding view and also change the difficulty of the trek to Moderate to Hard. We started around 4:10 AM in the Morning on 3rd Jan 2017 and reached the summit just at the time of Sunrise. The temperature was harsh, on the thermometer, the mercury dropped to -10¬∞ C. Few people in my group quit in the middle and at the last stretch, the trekking was left behind and now we were doing the Mountaineering to reach the top. We were walking on the ridge and this ridge shakes the confidence of almost 90% of trekkers in my group. After this trek, I broke my past record of highest altitude, longest distance in mountains & coldest temperature.
