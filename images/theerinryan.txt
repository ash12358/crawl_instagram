I am so proud if my mini-roses...and a good shared lesson for those who may be going through a difficult time. The Leaf of Hope These roses were undervalued. They had bloomed, unfolding beautifully talented buds that turned into pink roses for all to love and enjoy. However, they might have been too overworked  by the energy of those peering at them, that they too, like everything else, began to wilt...and slowly look as though they were ready to be tossed. I however, was loyal. I stood by these roses as I watched them lose their bloom. I watered them and cut off their stems to give them a chance of regrowth. But this was only due to my persistence, my willingness to be assertive when the rose bush needed me to be most. Clearly without the ability to ask...or say that it had life left in it. All signs pointed to death. But I watered it...and gave it sun instead of giving up on all its hard work to provide me blooms...I nurtured it. And today...it provided me a leaf...a little one...but still it is a healthy little growing leaf. I see it as the leaf of hope. That no matter the circumstance you may find yourself in, there will always be someone, besides yourself, that will believe in your value. You may know them now....or perhaps they have yet to introduce themselves...just do not give up hope. This rose bush gave me a leaf, an extension of itself to communicate to me that not all my dedication was lost. BE this rose bush...and rise again.
