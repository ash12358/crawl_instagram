@miss.chezza & @kakookie_cosplay both nominated me to share ten random facts about me. I am terrible being put on the spot, but imma gonna do it anyways. 😜 So here are my ten random facts about me: 💎 1. Everyone knows how much I love coffee, but did you know I actually hate the taste of unsweetened coffee.  #CoffeeIsLife 💎 2. I used to be an alcoholic. But one day I got really sick & had to go to emergency. So without anyone’s help, I overcame my own two year addiction by healthy eating, working out, modeling & picking back up my childhood hobby of photography. For my own reasons I stayed 100% sober for 5 years. After 5 years I wanted to let myself see if I had gained back my control over myself.  I’m happy to say I have, and it’s liberating. #HavingControlIsSexy 💎 3. I took sewing lessons at age 10 and loved it. But I become bored with it & thought it would be a useless skill to have in life. At age 27, I had to reteach myself how to sew. . . (Had some help from fellow Cosplayers to relearn) #Whoops 💎 4. I’m allergic to cats. . . Yet I own one.  I honestly couldn’t live without having a pet. #Cats 💎 5. I’m allergic to peas, beans, peanuts & carrots. Oh and six kinds of trees. Dust, mold & am lactose intolerant 🤷‍♀️ #ShouldLiveInABubble 💎 6. I never learnt how to drive, driving terrifies me. #BusLife 💎 7. I took collage course in makeup artistry and am a certified makeup artist. #MakeupAddict 💎 8. The only gaming systems I’ve ever enjoyed gaming on were an N64 & the old grey brick Gameboys.  I lived most of my childhood playing on those plus, building LEGO.  #Nintendo 💎 9. I get bored with hobbies when I master a skill.  So i try to have as many creative outlets as possible so I almost forget what I’ve learned and it stays fresh and fun for me.  I love being challenged with art.  #AlwaysLearning 💎 10. I’m a perfectionist. And for somethings in Cosplay & in life you just need to “wing it” which is so incredibly hard for me to do.  I want everything I create or do to be PERFECT 👌🏻 but sometimes I should just do what I can & improve it later.  #LetItGo
