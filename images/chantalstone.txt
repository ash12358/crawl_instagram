Day 8 of #21daysinmyartworld: Inspiration. For me that's kind of a weird question. Any artist will say they find inspiration in nature, freedom, love, music, conflict, etc, etc....and for me that's all true too. But there's more to it than just that. I'm an abstract painter. Expressionist, Intuitive, sometimes Gestural. But none of those words explain or define my art. My abstract work is mostly inspired by abstract thoughts and ideas, such as freedom, the expanding universe, spirituality, vague memories or fleeting dreams. But i'm also inspired by contradictions: dark vs light self expression vs conformity rejection vs unconditional love loneliness vs solitude abundance vs scarcity what do these things look like? i find that with abstract art people will often say "oh i see a tree", "i see a cloud", etc.... but most of the time my art is none of those things. it's abstract. it's not necessarily meant to be ANY of those things. it just is. so then the work becomes: intention vs interpretation but i don't feel like my intention is something that needs to be known. i know it, but art is personal so i'm the only one who needs to know it. the second exercise of art is viewing. so my intention and inspiration become less important. . . . #art #artdaily #abstractexpressionism #abstractart #gesturalabstraction #artistsoninstagram #artislife #614art #artmakescbus #columbusartist #artcollective #flashesofdelight #flaming_abstracts #artistic_dome #carveouttimeforart #modernart #contemporaryart #decorativeart #dscolor #dslooking #harpersgalleria #makersmovement #thinkabouttheart #artcollector #instaabstract #intuitiveart #artsnewss #intuitiveart #intuitivepainting #harpersgalleria
