75 years ago today, Executive Order 9066 was signed and issued by FDR which started the process of the internment of over 100,000 Japanese Americans during WWII. 80,000 of them were native-born citizens. - They were spread out in 10 hastily built camps, one of which was at #Manzanar which is just south of Independence, CA. I make it a point to stop by every time I return from Mammoth to pay my respects and to reflect on this dark period of American History. This is a topic that is rarely discussed in our schools and it's a story that should be shared. - This past week after my trip to Mammoth, I made a bigger effort to visit the site since the anniversary of EO #9066 was coming up. There are a lot of stories here at their museum and if you get a chance to come here, please take the self-guided car tour around the camp and read along as you go. Also, speak to one of the park rangers on site. Unlike other national parks, these rangers don't sit in a class or read a book to learn about Manzanar.
