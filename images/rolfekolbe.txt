zAPpo.... SuperheroEdit#2 A phone edit challenge! #phoneart First of all phone photography to get the concept and composition! Then the hard work! STEP1 #snapseed app to tune image; correct brightness, ambience and contrast then selective adjust to make white background brighter and desaturated to be really white! STEP2: #PStouch to make layers and delete a few parts of the original layer (junk in the background) and merge with a white background layer. also a little bit of an edit to desensitise photo! Then also added the Z via text layer and then warping to match what it should look like plus a glow to the  Z to blend a big better. STEP3:  #colorrange app to turn to black and white and then "in paint" the shorts and cape, which were blue and red, then changed hues to make them dark blue and green. STEP4: #repix to then use posterise and silk to get the simplistic desired effect!! STEP5: Instagram to finish up! Yeah I love xpro! VOILA!
