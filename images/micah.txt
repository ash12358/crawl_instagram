Miso black cod with buttered brussel sprouts. It’s been a long time since I went back for seconds with my own food, but holy crap this is good. I based it on the Nobu recipe. Ended up doing: 3 or so tablespoons of red miso, 1/4 cup swerve, 2 TB of white white vinegar and 1 TB roasted seasoned oil. Put it all in a sauce pan and stir until the sugar is melted and the miso is incorporated. Set aside until room temp. Season fish with a bit of salt and then pour over the miso mixture. Let stand like 15 min. Quarter sheet with aluminum foil. Hi broil for 5 min, then spin 180 degrees and broil for another 5 min or so. Once flakey, pull all the pin bones (super easy). Brussel sprouts were out of a bag and I boiled and mixed with butter. Boom. 30 min total. My favorite fish dish so far.
