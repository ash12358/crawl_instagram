Homemade Rose Water I made rose water spray for the first day of 100 days of art to help me get through the summer heat. It turned out really well. This can be used all over your body or on your face for a toner and refresher. 1. Take all the petals off your fading roses. 2. Rinse the residue off them in lukewarm eater. 3. Boil them in distilled water until they are blanched. 4. Strain the petals out until you have pure rose water. 5. Add more distilled water and a few drops of an essential oil that you've tested on your skin in diluted water in advance. 6. Lavender, jasmine, and rose oils all work well for this project. 7.I added a few drops of jasmine oil because it is my favorite essential oil and it's super girly. 8. Put it in a spray bottle and keep it chilled in your fridge. 9. Spritz it on your face or body every day.
