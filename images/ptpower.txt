19 May 2018 Royal Albert Hall When I set out for my walk on my last full day in London, it occurred to me to check to see where Royal Albert Hall was. Royal Albert Hall, while it has been the venue for many, many concerts since it was opened in 1871, is known to me and many people for a concert that actually occurred at Manchester Free Trade Hall a little over a week earlier. Bob Dylan, who had come to fame as a "folk" musician, was performing his world tour with a band, and during the Manchester show, just prior to performing "Like A Rolling Stone", someone in the audience shouted out, "Judas!" Dylan, strumming his guitar and tapping his foot to crank up the song, responded, "I don't believe you... you're a liar!" As a collector of rare Dylan recordings years ago, I obtained a copy of the electric half of the Manchester show (at that time, he performed an acoustic first half before having the band join him for the second half) which for many years had been mislabeled as having been performed at Royal Albert Hall.
